Portfolio review

Upload your CAS. We'll send a written note in 72 hours.

For investors considering a change of distributor, or simply a second opinion. The review is free and comes with no onboarding obligation. Forward your Consolidated Account Statement from CAMS or KFintech to review@anvithbizcap.in and we'll respond with a one-page summary.

What we look at

Category allocation & overlap

Your equity-debt split, fund-level overlap, and whether the top 10 holdings across your schemes are effectively the same stocks.

What we flag

Expense drag & category drift

Schemes whose TER has crept up, or whose strategy has drifted from the original mandate. Both quietly cost you return.

What we don't do

Cold push

We won't recommend you switch to regular plans if you're holding well-chosen direct plans; won't advise churn for commission. The review is a one-pager, not a sales call.

Grievance redressal

A concern? We'd rather hear it.

Indian regulated intermediaries are required to publish a clear grievance redressal mechanism. Here is ours. If at any stage you are not satisfied with the resolution, SEBI's SCORES portal and the Online Dispute Resolution (ODR) framework are your next escalation paths.

Step 1

Talk to your RM

Most concerns are resolved the same day. Your RM's direct line and email are in your onboarding pack.

Step 2

Raise a formal grievance

Email our Compliance & Grievance Officer, Raghav Nair, at grievance@anvithbizcap.in. Acknowledged within 2 business days, resolved within 30.

Step 3

Escalate to SEBI SCORES / ODR

If unresolved within 30 days, or you are dissatisfied with the resolution: scores.sebi.gov.in or the Online Dispute Resolution portal smartodr.in.

Red-flag: payment solicitation. AnvithBizCap will never ask you to transfer money to a personal account or any account not in the name of an AMC, clearing corporation, bank issuer or SEBI-registered OBPP. If anyone claiming to represent us asks you for such a transfer, do not proceed — and report it to grievance@anvithbizcap.in immediately.
