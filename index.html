Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks, read all scheme-related documents carefully. The past performance of mutual fund schemes is not an indicator of future performance. AnvithBizCap does not provide investment advice. Information on this website is for general education and product disclosure only and must not be construed as a recommendation. Investors should consult their tax adviser and read the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) before investing.

NBFC deposits are not insured. Bank fixed deposits are covered under DICGC up to ₹5 lakh per depositor per bank (principal + interest). NBFC public deposits are not covered under any deposit-insurance scheme and the investor carries the full credit risk of the NBFC issuer.

Corporate bonds displayed on this site are distributed through a SEBI-registered Online Bond Platform Provider (OBPP) partner. AnvithBizCap does not itself operate an OBPP. Investor funds route directly to the exchange clearing corporation.

Specialized Investment Funds (SIFs) are a regulated asset class introduced by SEBI in 2025, subject to a minimum investment of ₹10 lakh per PAN across all SIF strategies of an AMC. SIFs may use derivatives and short positions and carry a higher risk than traditional mutual funds. Only NISM Series-XIII certified personnel may distribute SIFs.

Sovereign Gold Bonds are issued by the Reserve Bank of India on behalf of the Government of India. Availability is subject to RBI tranche notifications. Secondary-market purchases do not qualify for capital-gains exemption on maturity.

We never solicit payments to any personal account. Please verify our ARN and this domain before transacting. In case of any complaint not resolved within 30 days, you may escalate to SEBI SCORES at scores.sebi.gov.in or to the Online Dispute Resolution portal at smartodr.in.
