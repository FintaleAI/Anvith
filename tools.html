06 · Disclosure

Regular vs Direct

The most honest calculator on this page. The TER differential between regular and direct plans of the same scheme compounds — over 20 years on a ₹10 L portfolio, it can reach a meaningful sum. We distribute regular plans and we want you to see what that costs.

ScenarioDirect planRegular plan (via us)Differential over 20 years
₹5 L one-time, flexi-cap, 12% / 11.2% net ₹48.2 L ₹42.1 L − ₹6.1 L
₹20,000/mo SIP, flexi-cap, 12% / 11.2% net ₹1.99 Cr ₹1.83 Cr − ₹16.1 L
₹10 L one-time, debt fund, 8% / 7.5% net ₹46.6 L ₹42.5 L − ₹4.1 L

Illustrative. Actual TER differential varies by AMC and category — ranging from 25 bps (debt) to 100 bps (small-cap equity). If you value self-research and can sustain review discipline for 20 years, direct plans with a SEBI RIA partnership may be the better choice. We are happy to introduce you.

What you get in a regular plan with us

  • Scheme shortlisting from a 7-screen universe
  • SIP / STP / SWP execution & mandate management
  • Quarterly portfolio review with written notes
  • Maturity calendars, capital gains summary, tax extracts
  • Grievance handling and issuer-side escalation
  • A named RM you can call before 6pm

When to choose direct with a flat-fee RIA

  • Portfolio above ₹1 Cr (fee leverage improves)
  • Willingness to research scheme fundamentals yourself
  • Preference for one-time fee over ongoing TER drag
  • Low service intensity required
  • Clear separation between advisory and distribution desired
